      <section class="section principle-section" id="principle">
        <div class="section-heading">
          <p class="eyebrow">Measurement Principle</p>
          <h2>检测原理</h2>
        </div>
        <div class="principle-layout">
          <div class="principle-visual" aria-label="RUF-100 超声波测量路径动态示意">
            <div class="principle-flow-label" aria-hidden="true">流向</div>
            <img src="安装图2.png" alt="RUF-100 外夹式超声波流量计超声波测量路径示意" />
            <span class="sensor-label sensor-a">A</span>
            <span class="sensor-label sensor-b">B</span>
            <span class="sonic-beam beam-down"></span>
            <span class="sonic-beam beam-up"></span>
            <span class="sonic-ripple ripple-a"></span>
            <span class="sonic-ripple ripple-b"></span>
          </div>
          <div class="principle-copy">
            <p>
              RUF-100 通过外夹式传感器在管壁外发射与接收超声波信号，测量 A 点到 B 点双向传播的时间差。流体速度变化时，顺流与逆流传播时间产生差异，设备据此换算瞬时流量与累计流量。
            </p>
            <div class="principle-cards">
              <article>
                <h3>低流量</h3>
